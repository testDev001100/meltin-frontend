Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the service free?
A: Yes, Meltin' is a free service. Group matching is done through a survey, and no additional fees are charged.
Q: How is the group matching done?
A: Group matching is done automatically based on the results of the survey, aligning users with others who share similar traits. The survey is simple, and it helps ensure smooth group activities.
Q: Do I need to sign up?
A: Yes, signing up is necessary. We require basic user identification (ID, password, name, student number) to store survey responses and team matching results for personalized features like member pages and match history.
Q: How long does the survey take?
A: The survey takes about 5 minutes to complete. It's designed to be quick and easy, so you can participate without any hassle.
Q: When can I check my group matching results?
Once all participants complete the survey, the admin will review the results and click the 'Group Matching' button to run the algorithm. The results will then be announced to participants.
Q: Can I change my matched group?
A: Group matching is a core feature, so changing groups is not possible. However, if there is an issue with your match, please contact our support team.
Q: Is it available on mobile?
A: Yes, Meltin' is available on both PC and mobile. The mobile version offers a more intuitive and streamlined interface.
